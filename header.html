HUMAN ACTIVITY RECOGNITION

Human Activity Recognition (HAR), Is a machine learning web application designed to detect falls in real-time and trigger an alert. HAR's centre focus for the duration of the project was using convolutional neural networks as a means to detect falls but this proved unsuccessful. Furthermore, it was in the end proven that HAAR Cascade Classifier would be more feasible at detecting the fall. This was the approach which was adopted from a multitude of sources to detect the fall.


Human Activity Recognition is aimed at contributing to the betterment of an ageing Irish population through effective fall detection,
which contributes to the attributes of independence, safety and comfort. Removing the need to wear sensors enables a feeling of independence from technology and allows you to move on with your day.
It also feels more comfortable. Finally, the detection algorithm should be performant enough to safeguard in falls and guarantee a high degree of prediction capability. However this may not be the end product.
